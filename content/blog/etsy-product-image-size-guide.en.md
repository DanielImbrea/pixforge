Etsy is a visual marketplace built for makers, vintage sellers, and digital artists. Strict white-background rules like Amazon do not apply — **lifestyle, texture, and story** often outperform sterile pack shots. But dimension rules still matter: soft photos lose zoom, badly cropped thumbnails kill click-through, and 8 MB uploads timeout on mobile admin.

This guide covers **Etsy-specific** image sizes for 2026: listing photo dimensions, ten-slot strategy, thumbnail behavior, 4:3 and 4:5 ratios, shop banner and icon, handmade aesthetic, formats, compression, and prep workflow. For general crop technique, see [crop without losing quality](/en/blog/crop-image-without-losing-quality); for Amazon white-background rules, see [Amazon product image requirements](/en/blog/amazon-product-image-requirements).

## Why Etsy image dimensions matter

Etsy's app and web crop listing photos differently in search grids, favorites, and ads. The **first photo** is your thumbnail everywhere — if the product sits in the corner of a horizontal frame, mobile search may crop it out entirely.

Etsy also enables buyer zoom on listing pages. Below recommended pixel counts, zoom shows softness — bad for jewelry, prints, and fabric texture where detail sells.

## Recommended listing photo size: 2000+ pixels

Etsy recommends listing photos with **at least 2000 pixels on the shortest side**. Practical exports:

| Ratio | Example dimensions | Best for |
|-------|-------------------|----------|
| 4:3 horizontal | 2700×2025 px | Wide products, flat lays, room scenes |
| 4:5 vertical | 2000×2500 px | Tall products, portraits of items on model |
| 1:1 square | 2000×2000 px | Icons, mugs, symmetric crafts |

Export at 2000+ on the **shortest side**, not just long edge — Etsy guidance emphasizes minimum shortest-side pixels.

## The ten-photo slot strategy

Ten photos per listing — use them as a story:

| Slot | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| 1 | Thumbnail hero — clearest product view, centered |
| 2 | Alternate angle or detail |
| 3 | Scale reference (hand, coin, ruler) |
| 4 | Lifestyle in use or styled scene |
| 5 | Process / handmade proof |
| 6–10 | Variants, packaging, gift context, size chart |

Slot one must match what ships — Etsy's policies penalize misleading thumbnails.

## Thumbnail and search grid safe zones

Keep the product in the **center 80%** of the frame. Etsy search on mobile uses square-ish crops from rectangular uploads. Leave margin around edges for UI overlays.

After [crop](/en/blog/crop-image-without-losing-quality), preview at small size — squint test mimics search scroll.

## Handmade aesthetic vs white-background catalog

Unlike Amazon, Etsy rewards context:

- Wooden tables, linen backdrops, natural light.
- Process shots — workshop, kiln, sewing machine.
- Imperfect authenticity beats sterile white for many categories.

Still remove distracting clutter with selective [background removal](/en/blog/remove-background-from-product-photos) when a busy room competes with the product — not mandatory white flatten.

## JPG vs PNG on Etsy

**JPEG** — Default for physical product photography. Upload faster; sufficient for fabric, ceramics, prints photographed.

**PNG** — Digital downloads (clip art, printables), transparent sticker previews, sharp flat graphics.

Digital sellers: deliver PNG/PDF separately; listing preview can be JPG of mockup.

## File size limits and compression

Etsy max: **20 MB** per image, **3000 pixels** minimum recommendation on shortest side in some docs — 2000 px remains safe baseline; higher for print-on-demand art.

Compress with [Image Compressor](/en/image-compressor) after resize — target 300 KB–1.5 MB per listing photo. Details: [compress product images](/en/blog/compress-product-images-without-losing-quality).

## Shop banner and icon sizes

**Shop icon:** 500×500 px upload — displays circular. Keep logo centered; corners clip.

**Shop banner (big):** 1600×400 px (4:1). Text and products in center safe zone — edges crop on mobile.

**Team / about photos:** Follow listing dimension guidance for consistency.

Verify [Etsy Seller Handbook](https://www.etsy.com/seller-handbook) for updates — dimensions occasionally shift.

## Vertical crop from horizontal sources

Phone photos often arrive 16:9 or 3:2 landscape. For 4:5 listing ratio:

1. [Crop](/en/image-cropper) to 4:5 with product centered.
2. [Resize](/en/image-resizer) shortest side to 2000 px.
3. Compress once.

For Pinterest cross-promotion, also export 2:3 — see [Pinterest image size guide](/en/blog/pinterest-image-size-guide).

## Etsy Shop product images (brief)

Etsy Shop (on-site checkout) uses listing photos — same prep. External ads may pull slot one — keep it high contrast and readable small.

## Multi-platform sellers

Same SKU on Etsy, Shopify, and Amazon needs **three export presets**:

- Amazon: white main 1600+ square — [Amazon guide](/en/blog/amazon-product-image-requirements).
- Shopify: 2048 square — [Shopify guide](/en/blog/shopify-product-image-size).
- Etsy: 2000+ 4:5 lifestyle hero.

Master PSD with smart objects saves seasonal updates.

## Common Etsy image mistakes

**Blurry slot one from Instagram re-save** — Export from original files.

**Product in corner** — Thumbnail crops it out.

**All ten photos identical angle** — Wasted story slots.

**Oversized uncompressed PNG** — Mobile upload fails.

**Misleading slot one** — Different color/material than shipped item.
